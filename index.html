Highball

Paloma

  • 2 oz Tequila blanco or mezcal
  • 1/2 oz Lime juice
  • 4 oz Grapefruit soda
  • Add directly in glass over ice and stir gently for 5–10 seconds

Fresh, bright, and easy to enjoy throughout the reception.

Signature Cocktail

Soft texture, smoky depth, wedding-ready presentation

Clarified

Clarified Milk Cocktail — Mezcal

  • 1.5 oz Mezcal
  • 3/4 oz Lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz Simple syrup
  • 1/2 oz Orange liqueur
  • 1 oz milk for clarification process
  • Serve cold over a large cube or neat after clarification

Smooth, refined, and elegant with a subtle smoky finish. Replace these proportions with your original clarified mezcal recipe if you had a specific version before.

Easy Favorite

Relaxed, familiar, and approachable

Classic Mixed Drink

Rum & Coke

  • 2 oz Rum
  • 4 oz Coke
  • Lime wedge optional
  • Add directly in glass over ice and stir gently for 5–10 seconds

A comfortable crowd-pleaser for guests who want something simple and familiar.
